iHow the suggestions are made

The rubric, in three parts.

i. Income replacement

Term life uses 8–10x annual income plus liabilities, adjusted for years to expected financial independence of dependents.

ii. Floor by tier

Health cover floors scale by city tier (Tier-1 metro requires 1.5–2x Tier-2 floor). Family medical history adjusts the floor upward.

iii. Disability gap

Disability cover targets 60% earned-income replacement for a 10-year window, the conservative end of actuarial norms.

iiImportant caveats

What this tool does not do.

The Insurance Advisor does not sell, recommend, or commission policies from any provider. Output figures are heuristics intended for orientation, not actuarial certifications. Policy structure, exclusions, claim history, and provider reputation matter as much as cover quantum — the tool does not evaluate those. For decisions involving significant premium commitments, speak with a SEBI-registered investment adviser or an IRDAI-registered insurance broker.
