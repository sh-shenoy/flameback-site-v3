Personal, diagnostic, recommendation-driven. Use them with a real portfolio, a real family situation, or a real question. They give honest answers because they were built for honest use.

Portfolio doctor.

A diagnostic for any portfolio. Paste holdings, get a frank read.

Paste your holdings — from a PMS, a mutual fund book, or a self-directed account — and the tool returns a structured read across concentration, factor exposure, expense drag, tax composition, and rebalancing gap. Honest signals, no upselling.
