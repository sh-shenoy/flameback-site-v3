Investment Advisory Agreement

1. This agreement is entered into between Aarav (“the Client”) and Flameback Capital Pvt. Ltd., a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser (RIA No. INA000017xxx), governing the advisory relationship for the portfolio “My growth bet”.

Scope of advice

2. Flameback shall provide investment advice matched to the Client's risk profile and stated objectives. The relationship is advisory: the Client approves every trade before it executes. Flameback holds no discretionary authority over the Client's funds or securities.

Fees

3. A one-time onboarding fee of ₹5,000 applies, fully offset against future advisory fees. The recurring advisory fee is 0.9% of assets under advice per annum, billed on the cadence the Client selects, in line with SEBI's fee regulations for Investment Advisers.

No assured returns

4. Investments are subject to market risk. Flameback makes no representation or guarantee of returns. Past performance and backtested figures are not indicative of future results.

Conflicts & disclosures

5. Flameback receives no commissions, referral fees, or distribution incentives from any product manufacturer. The Adviser's only remuneration is the fee paid directly by the Client.

Suitability & records

6. Advice is based on the information the Client has provided through profiling. The Client undertakes to keep this information current. Flameback retains all records and this signed agreement for the period required under SEBI regulations.

Termination

7. Either party may terminate this agreement with written notice. On termination, advice ceases; the Client's holdings remain in the Client's own broker account throughout.

Governing law

8. This agreement is governed by the laws of India and subject to the jurisdiction of the courts at Mumbai. By signing, the Client confirms they have read and understood every clause above.
