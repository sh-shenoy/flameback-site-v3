Suggested adjustments — mutual funds

i

Consolidate the correlated four. Parag Parikh, Mirae L&M, Axis Bluechip, and Mirae ELSS run at > 0.88 average pairwise correlation. They are not four bets; they are one bet held four times. Retain Parag Parikh as the flexi-cap anchor; exit Axis Bluechip; switch Mirae L&M and Mirae ELSS to Direct plans at lower TER.

ii

Re-allocate the small-cap weight down the risk curve. SBI Small Cap is the single largest contributor to off-profile volatility. Trimming it by 50–60% and parking the freed capital in HDFC Mid Cap (in profile, lower TER, comparable performance) brings realised vol back toward the moderate band.

iii

Replace Mirae ELSS — lock-in expired. The 3-year ELSS lock-in is past. The fund is underperforming its benchmark and the tax benefit is locked in already. Redirect future ELSS allocation to a Direct plan with stronger 3- and 5-year numbers.

iv

TER target: 0.85%. Switching Regular plans to Direct on three funds, plus the consolidation above, brings aggregate TER from 1.45% to an estimated 0.85% — a recurring annual saving of ~₹1,44,000 on this folio.

iMethodology

How the engine works.

The factor-model engine runs nine analytical modules against the holdings + risk-profile inputs. Scores are weighted by a transparent rubric. Factor exposures are computed against the NSE 500 universe; mutual-fund factor decomposition uses 60-month rolling regressions on category benchmarks.

i. Concentration

Top-10 weight, single-name max, sector max, Herfindahl index. Scored against typical large-cap PMS distributions.

ii. Factor exposure

Each name attributed to size, value, momentum, quality, and low-vol factors via 60-month rolling regressions.

iii. Correlation & diversification

Effective number of bets from the cross-correlation matrix. Surfaces funds doing the same job.

iv. Risk-profile fit

Realised vol of the portfolio compared against the band implied by the user’s questionnaire answers.

v. Expense drag

Embedded TER across mutual funds + PMS, plus implied trading cost when turnover data is available.

vi. Tax position

Trade-history-implied STCG/LTCG composition versus an idealised aged portfolio.

iiLimitations

What this tool does not do.

The diagnostic is a structural read at a point in time. It does not forecast returns, predict which positions will outperform, or name specific replacements. It is not a substitute for a personalised conversation with a SEBI-registered investment adviser; it works only from rule-based heuristics, factor-model regressions, and the holdings and risk profile you provide. For decisions involving meaningful capital, we recommend a personal consultation.
